- Players control "The Clockwork Crew," a squad of dancers who have synchronized their acrobatic movements to the rhythm of the music.
- Each level features a different music track, and players must hit notes in time to execute moves that keep them ahead of their pursuers.
- The game features an in-game economy where players earn "Beat Coins" by successfully performing dance sequences. These Beat Coins can be used to upgrade gear, unlock new dance moves, and buy temporary power-ups.

- Unique power-ups can temporarily alter the gameplay; for example, "Tempo Turbo" makes the crew move faster, and "Harmony Hurdle" creates obstacles for the guards.
- Leadership Mechanics: The player must decide which member of The Clockwork Crew takes the lead during different parts of a track to utilize their unique skills optimally.

Player Challenge
- Mastering rhythm to make precise, in-time moves that ensure escape.
- Managing resources effectively by earning and spending Beat Coins on crucial upgrades or power-ups.
- Quickly adapting to changing roles and deciding when to press the advantage or play defensively based on the rhythm and game situation.
- Leading the team effectively by switching between crew members to utilize their specific strengths strategically.

Design Goals
Engaging: By incorporating a unique rhythm-based chase mechanic, this game challenges players to hone their timing skills while leading a team in action-packed sequences.
Dynamic: Frequently switching between roles of chasing and being chased provides variety and keeps the gameplay fresh and unpredictable.
Strategic Depth: Combining rhythm elements with meticulous resource management offers players both immediate and long-term decisions to consider as they progress through levels.
Immersive: The steampunk setting, combined with a compelling soundtrack, ties the thematic elements of mechanics and gears into a cohesive, immersive gaming experience.

In "Rhythm Runaway," players engage with an immersive steampunk universe through intuitive rhythm-based mechanics combined with strategic team management.
